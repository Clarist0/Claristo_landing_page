What Claristo does

Intelligent tools.
Real results.

Built around the real challenges job seekers face — not the ones career advisors imagine.

✦
AI CV Tailoring, Generation & Cover Letters

Paste any job URL and Claristo rewrites your CV to match that role instantly. Building from scratch? Answer a few questions and Claristo builds it for you. Cover letters included, every time.

📊
Application Fit Score

Know exactly how competitive your application is before you hit send. Claristo scores your CV against the job description — keyword alignment, experience relevance, tone — so you apply with confidence, not guesswork.

✉️
Personalised Recruiter Outreach

Most candidates apply and wait. Claristo generates tailored outreach messages to hiring managers and recruiters — specific to the role, the company, and your background. Stand out before the interview even begins.

👻
Ghost Job Detection

Not every job posting is a real opportunity. Claristo analyses multiple signals to flag roles with low hiring activity before you spend time applying.

  • How long the role has been posted
  • Whether it's been reposted multiple times
  • Company hiring activity across other roles
  • Posting patterns that suggest a filled or frozen position
💡
Job Insights & Tailored Suggestions

Understand the full picture of every role before you apply. Claristo surfaces meaningful insights and suggests targeted actions — from CV tweaks to outreach strategies — specific to that application.

🎯
Skills Gap Analysis

Discover exactly what employers in your target field are looking for and where your profile falls short. Claristo maps the gap and recommends courses to close it fast.
