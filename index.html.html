FAQ

Quick answers.

01 Is Claristo free? ▼

Claristo will offer a free trial when it launches — no credit card required. Our full pricing will be announced closer to launch, but we're committed to making it accessible. Join the waitlist to be the first to know.

02 Who is Claristo for? ▼

Claristo is built for students, graduates, career switchers, and anyone actively or passively looking for their next role. Whether you're applying for your first job or your tenth, Claristo helps every application count.

03 Will there be mobile support? ▼

Yes. Claristo is being built to work seamlessly across desktop and mobile from day one. You'll be able to import your CV, tailor applications, and track your job search from your phone.

04 When does Claristo launch? ▼

We're currently in development and will be launching early access soon. Joining the waitlist guarantees you a spot in the first wave — and founding users will receive special perks unavailable after launch.

05 Is my CV data safe? ▼

Absolutely. Your data is encrypted, never sold, and never shared with employers or third parties. You own your CV — always. Privacy is a core principle at Claristo, not an afterthought.
